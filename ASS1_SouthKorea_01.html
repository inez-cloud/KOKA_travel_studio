CITY OVERVIEW

Seoul, the capital of South Korea, is a bustling metropolis that blends rich history with modern innovation. Known for its impressive skyline, historic palaces, and vibrant neighborhoods, the city offers a diverse cultural experience.

The city is rich in cultural heritage, featuring palaces, temples, and traditional hanok houses. Contemporary art, K-pop, and festivals reflect its dynamic cultural landscape. Seoul is also a hub for technology, fashion, and cuisine, famous for dishes like kimchi and bibimbap. Seoul’s dynamic atmosphere, combined with its deep-rooted traditions, makes it a captivating destination for travelers and locals alike.

Audeum – Audio Museum

ARCHITECTURAL FEATURES

The Audeum, is a museum that goes beyond the traditional concept of a museum. The exterior design uses vertically overlapping aluminum louvers to create changing shadows, where light and shadow meet between the louvers, producing a beautiful effect like sunlight in a forest. The use of aluminum pipes mimics the changes in natural light, randomly arranging the pipes to harmonize natural disorder and order, emphasizing the beauty of light that changes according to weather, time, and season. Additionally, the bright aluminum pipes envelop the entire building, allowing the exterior to show new expressions according to the seasons, incorporating the random characteristics of the urban environment and nature into the architecture.

The interior of the exhibition rooms uses wood, adopting a detail we call ‘wood drape,’ which differs from typical wood finishes, emphasizing the softness and naturalness of the wood. The wood finish is designed with acoustics in mind, creating a space that stimulates human senses and fosters a sense of closeness to nature.

ACTIVITIES

The Audeum is a space where you can experience not only visual elements but also sound, light, wind, and fragrance, engaging all the senses. This place, where you can experience a new sensory world, goes beyond a museum, providing a special healing and sensory experience. It is destined to become a symbolic place of the new era.
